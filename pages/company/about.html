We're on a mission to make accepting cryptocurrency as easy as accepting a credit card. Secure, instant, and accessible for every business.

Our Mission

XCoinGateway was founded with a single belief: crypto payments should be simple. We saw businesses struggling with fragmented blockchain infrastructure, volatile settlements, and complex integrations — and we set out to solve it.

Today, we provide a unified payment gateway supporting 8 blockchains and 50+ tokens, with all payments automatically converting to USDT for zero volatility risk. Our merchants can focus on growth while we handle the blockchain complexity.

What drives us

Our core principles guide every product decision and engineering choice.

🔒

Security First

AES-256 key encryption, HMAC-signed webhooks, role-based access control, and immutable audit logs protect every transaction.

⚡

Speed & Reliability

Multiple API providers per chain with automatic failover ensure 99.9% uptime. Payments are detected and confirmed in real-time.

🧩

Developer Experience

Clean REST APIs, comprehensive SDKs, webhook testing tools, and a sandbox environment — integrate in minutes, not weeks.

📊

Transparency

No hidden fees, clear pricing tiers, full transaction breakdowns, and real-time status monitoring for complete visibility.

🌐

Multi-Chain Native

Built from the ground up for multi-chain support — not bolted on. Ethereum, BNB, Polygon, Avalanche, Tron, Solana, Bitcoin, and XRP.

🤝

Merchant-Centric

Every feature is designed around what merchants need: simple onboarding, automatic settlements, dispute resolution, and analytics.
