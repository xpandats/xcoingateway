Protect your customers and your business with our built-in dispute and refund system. Transparent deadlines, automated fund holds, and clear resolution workflows.

How disputes work

When a customer raises a dispute, the affected funds are automatically held. Both parties have clear deadlines to respond. The process is transparent, logged, and fair to everyone involved.

  • ✓Customers can report fraud or incorrect charges
  • ✓Disputed funds are automatically frozen — no unauthorized withdrawals
  • ✓Merchants receive notification and have a deadline to respond
  • ✓Admin reviews evidence from both parties
  • ✓Approved refunds are sent to the customer's original wallet
  • ✓Complete audit trail for every dispute
