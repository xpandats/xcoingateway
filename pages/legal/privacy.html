Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how XCoinGateway collects, uses, and safeguards your data.

Last Updated: March 28, 2026Effective: March 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, business name, and wallet address(es). For merchant accounts, we may also request business verification documents.

1.2 Transaction Data

We process and store transaction data including payment amounts, blockchain addresses, transaction hashes, timestamps, and payment status. This data is essential for providing payment processing services.

1.3 Technical Data

We automatically collect technical information including IP addresses, browser type, device information, operating system, and page visit timestamps. This data helps us maintain platform security and improve performance.

1.4 API Usage Data

For merchants using our API, we log API calls, request/response payloads (excluding sensitive data), and webhook delivery statuses for debugging, analytics, and rate limiting purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected information to:

  • Provide Services — Process payments, generate invoices, manage withdrawals, and settle transactions in USDT.
  • Security & Fraud Prevention — Detect suspicious activity, enforce velocity limits, manage wallet risk scores, and maintain blacklist/whitelist systems.
  • Communication — Send transaction receipts, webhook notifications, platform updates, and security alerts.
  • Compliance — Meet regulatory requirements including AML/KYC obligations where applicable.
  • Improvement — Analyze usage patterns to improve platform performance, reliability, and user experience.

3. Data Storage & Security

All sensitive data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption. Private keys are never stored in plaintext and are decrypted only during authorized signing operations. Our infrastructure uses:

  • HTTPS/TLS for all data in transit
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) for internal systems
  • Immutable, append-only audit logs for all administrative actions
  • Separate database instances per environment (development, staging, production)
  • Regular encrypted backups with hourly snapshots for production data

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Blockchain Networks — Transaction data is inherently public on blockchain networks when processing payments.
  • Third-Party Providers — Auto-sweep conversion providers receive transaction data necessary to execute token conversions.
  • Legal Requirements — We may disclose data when required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Request restriction of data processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@xcoingateway.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain transaction records and ledger data for a minimum of 7 years for audit and compliance purposes. Account data is retained for the duration of your account plus 2 years after closure. You may request deletion of non-essential data at any time.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for session management and authentication. For details on all cookies used, see our Cookie Policy.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email and/or a prominent notice on our platform at least 30 days before taking effect.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection team at privacy@xcoingateway.com or write to our registered address.
