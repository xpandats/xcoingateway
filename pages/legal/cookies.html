Cookie Policy

How XCoinGateway uses cookies and similar technologies to provide, secure, and improve our services.

Last Updated: March 28, 2026Effective: March 28, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognize your device and store information about your preferences or past actions.

2. Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the platform to function and cannot be disabled.

  • xcg_session — Maintains your authenticated session. Expires when you log out or after 24 hours of inactivity.
  • xcg_csrf — Prevents cross-site request forgery attacks. Generated per session.
  • xcg_theme — Stores your theme preference (light/dark). Persistent, 1 year.

Security Cookies

These cookies help protect against unauthorized access and suspicious activity.

  • xcg_device — Device fingerprint for detecting unauthorized login attempts. Persistent, 90 days.
  • xcg_rate — Tracks request frequency for rate limiting. Session-based.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with the platform so we can improve it.

  • xcg_analytics — Anonymous usage statistics (pages visited, features used). Persistent, 30 days.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or trackers.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from using authenticated features of the platform.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

4. Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy when we add or remove cookies. Updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

5. Contact

For questions about our cookie practices, contact privacy@xcoingateway.com.
